# 🚀 Welcome to D-FAB (Device, Fabrication, and Analog-Digital Design)
**D-FAB** is a student-led semiconductor research community at **DGIST**, dedicated to mastering the interconnected lifecycle of silicon technology.

## 🔬 Our Three Pillars
We believe true innovation comes from understanding the synergy between physical manufacturing and circuit architecture.

* **Fabrication & Process:** Exploring the 8 essential manufacturing steps, lithography, and next-generation cleanroom technologies.
* **Device Physics:** Analyzing semiconductor materials, transistor behavior (MOSFET/FinFET/GAA), and the physical limits of scaling.
* **Circuit Design:** Implementing robust Digital (Verilog) systems and High-precision Analog (IC) architectures based on device understanding.

## 📅 Key Activities
- **Full-Stack Study Groups:** Weekly sessions balanced across **Fabrication flows**, **Device physics**, and **Integrated circuits**.
- **Academic Paper Reviews:** In-depth analysis of the latest research from global conferences such as IEDM, VLSI, ISSCC, and IEEE.
- **Academic Conferences & Seminars:** Engaging with industry experts and attending major semiconductor symposia to bridge the gap between theory and the FAB.

## 👥 Our Mission
To cultivate well-rounded semiconductor experts who possess a holistic view—from the atomic level of a device to the complexity of a full-scale chip.
